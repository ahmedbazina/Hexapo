Features

Po is full of modern features that can be found in most robots with the ability to add more to it in the future

Bluetooth

Po can be controlled by connecting to it by bluetooth, sending commands and instructions

3D printable parts

Body parts are designed to fit 3D printers configurations, where parts are professionally designed to fit together. furthermore, broken parts can be replaced easily and economically by either buying the part from us or 3d printing it at your own cost.

Customizable

Po will have the facilities to be customizalbe by adding cameras and extra stuff to be announced later

Simple SDK 

At hexapo we use open source simple aurdino software development kit to operate Po and send instructions to it

WiFi

With the wifi features you will be able to connect to Po through wifi for better communication when developing and learning as well as playing

Chipset

Hexapo has its own bord design based on the arduino architecture to help run Po effectively

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HexaPo?

HexaPo is a DIY customizable robotic kit based on Arduino. It is designed for geeks and beginners who want to learn about robotics and electronic kits. It gives you the chance to go far away with your creativity and imagination to create whatever you want!

How does hexapo work?

HexaPo works and moves using ESP32 compatible board.

What's inside the box?

HexaPo Kit: 28 metal gear servos, ESP32 board, Lipo battery, battery charger and body parts with needed screws.

What can i do with hexapo?

You can customize and hack your own robot and build whatever you imagine with it by adding different sensors and camera module.
Can learn how robot works and learn about coding and electronic stuff.

Who should own this robot?

It was designed for hobbyists, beginners and geeks to give them the chance to build something creative.
(If you feel excited when you see a robot but don't know how it works...this is for you).

How long it takes to be assembled?

It takes 15-30 minutes depending on your experience with electronic components.

How can I control this robot?

HexaPo comes with open source PC software program with a simple interface.
Control the robot by building Bluetooth connection with the software program.

How long the bluetooth connection range is?

The robot uses Bluetooth HC-06 module which can reach up to 9 meters (30 feet)​.

How can I print my own parts and build the robot?

All the designs will be open source in STL file form after launching the campaign.
You will need to collect the electronic components yourself.

What if i don't have 3d printer?

You can buy the kit and it comes with plastic molded parts and the rest of electronic components.

How long does the battery last?

The capacity of the battery is 1000mAh lasts for 15 min of using.

How many i/o is available in controlling board?

We are using customized Arduino board that contains:

  • 28 GPIO
  • 24 servo channel
